# Voice Samples

Drop 3-5 of your best writing pieces here. These are used by `/content voice` to learn your writing patterns.

Good samples are:
- Pieces you're proud of
- Pieces that sound most like "you"
- Mix of formats if possible (blog post, thread, newsletter)

Name them descriptively:
- `best-blog-post.md`
- `favorite-thread.md`
- `newsletter-intro.md`

Then run:
```
/content voice brand/voice-samples/
```

The skill analyzes sentence rhythm, vocabulary, signature phrases, structural patterns, and formality level — then updates `brand/VOICE.md` with your profile.
